Description

The TM/TC Console is a software product for the visualization and recording of those industrial processes which are instrumented with telemetry and telecommand features.

This tool has been designed with customer experience to support both large and small scale installations with a broad range of readily available features. The installation, configuration, and visual tailoring of the Console is easy for anyone experienced with the installation and use of computer software. Console scripting with JavaScript permits the integration of instrumentation, and the development of novel controls.
